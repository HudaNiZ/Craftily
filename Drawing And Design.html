Learn Drawing
In Your Own Way

Drawing is an enjoyable artistic skill to learn and makes for a
great hobby. When you are first starting, the quality of your drawings
can feel like a major hurdle. You may think that you need
professional lessons to make something good, but this isn’t true.

By simply drawing for fun, you can save money and improve your skills.
To draw without classes, sketch in short lines, shade in shadows,
draw figures out of shapes, and practice as much as possible.
