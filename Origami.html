Origami

Have you ever folded a square sheet of paper into the shape of a bird?

That's origami, the Japanese art of paper folding. Origami comes from two Japanese words, ori, "fold," and kami, "paper".

Origami Instructions

Are you looking for origami instructions?
You've come to the right place!
Here you'll find out how to make many kinds of neat and ingenious items.
Our instructions consists of actual photographs of the folding process to help you along.

Some origami is incredibly complicated and takes a long time to learn,
while even small children can easily create simpler origami designs.
Every piece of finished origami, whether it's a dog or an umbrella or a box,
begins with a sheet of paper and specific folding techniques.
